Privacy Policy for ColorTap

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how ColorTap and Keaton Smiley handle information when you use the ColorTap app or submit a message through the support form on this website.

Information stored by the app

ColorTap is designed to keep your coloring progress on your device. This may include page progress, color choices, and related app state needed to reopen your work later.

This site does not describe user accounts, subscriptions, or public profile features because those are not part of the current public app experience described here.

Information you submit through support

If you use the support form on the home page, you may provide:

  • Your name, if you choose to include it
  • Your email address, if you want a reply
  • Your subject line and message

The form processor may also receive basic technical details associated with a submission, such as timestamps and IP address information.

How information is used

Information is used to:

  • Respond to support requests
  • Troubleshoot bugs and app issues
  • Improve app stability, support quality, and the overall user experience

Third-party services

Support form submissions on this website are processed by Formspree. Formspree may process submitted information under its own privacy and security practices. GitHub Pages may also process basic hosting-related request information when serving this site.

Retention

Support requests may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, keep records of support interactions, prevent abuse, or satisfy legal obligations.

Your choices

You can leave optional support form fields blank. If you do not want to share information through the support form, you can choose not to submit a request.

Children

If a child uses ColorTap, a parent or guardian should review app use and support submissions where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, use the support form on the ColorTap home page.
